numpy.polynomial.hermite_e.hermegauss(deg) [source]

Gauss-HermiteE quadrature.

Computes the sample points and weights for Gauss-HermiteE quadrature. These sample points and weights will correctly integrate polynomials of degree or less over the interval with the weight function .

Parameters:

deg : int

Number of sample points and weights. It must be >= 1.
Returns:

x : ndarray

1-D ndarray containing the sample points.

y : ndarray

1-D ndarray containing the weights.
