RopeJoint

Available since LÖVE 0.8.0
This type is not supported in earlier versions.

The RopeJoint enforces a maximum distance between two points on two bodies. It has no other effect.

Constructors

love.physics.newRopeJoint Creates a joint between two bodies that enforces a max distance between them. 0.8.0
